🚀 Meet the Visionary Behind Dominguez Tech Solutions

Pedro M. Dominguez

A passionate innovator with a deep love for technology, Pedro M. Dominguez has spent years navigating and learning about the digital world. Starting from humble beginnings—repairing phones and computers—Pedro has expanded his expertise to web development, AI automation, and IT consulting.

His mission? To democratize technology and make AI, automation, and digital solutions accessible to small businesses and entrepreneurs in Oklahoma City and beyond. Whether it's teaching a crash course on AI, developing a state-of-the-art chatbot, or guiding businesses through digital transformation, Pedro is dedicated to empowering others with knowledge.

"Technology shouldn't be a barrier—it should be an opportunity. If you have a vision, I’ll help you bring it to life." – Pedro
